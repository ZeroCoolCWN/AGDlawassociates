About Us

AGD LAW ASSOCIATES

Founded in 2016, AGD LAW ASSOCIATES is a law firm founded by young lawyers with strong commitment and willingness to grow PAN India. The members of AGD LAW ASSOCIATES come with a strong cultural heritage giving prime importance to the values and interests of the clients. AGD LAW ASSOCIATES has always been appreciated by its clients for being proactive exceeding clients expectations. AGD LAW ASSOCIATES offers complete legal solutions for domestic clients. The members of AGD LAW ASSOCIATES strictly believe in providing quality deliverables based on the clients requirements with commitment and honesty.

We Provide Legal Help

  • Legal Representation and Conducting proceedings Before the Hon’ble Madras High Court and Madurai Bench and before the Hon’ble District Courts and State and District Consumer forums, National Green Tribunal and Other Hon’ble Arbitration Tribunals, Debt Recovery Tribunals (Banking/Securitization), Income Tax Appellate Tribunal, National Company Law Tribunal etc and other Forums.

  • Administration of Office affairs and Co-ordination and delegation of duties among Junior Counsels/Paralegals

  • Documentation of Sale Agreements, Sale Deeds, Settlement Deeds, MOU’s, MOD’s, Work and Service Contracts, Hire Purchase Agreements, Personal Agreements, Rental Agreements, Construction Agreement, Joint Venture/Builder Agreements, Power of Attorney, Lease Agreements etc;

  • Currently assisting in a matter for Custody of Child
  • Appeared on behalf of Construction Companies, National Level – Supply Contractors, and Disputes arising out of Employer-Employee Contracts, Hire-Purchase Agreements, Work/Service Contracts

  • Appearance on behalf of Companies/Firms/Individual Clients in Civil, Criminal and Commercial Matters – Issues involving Transfer of Shares, Recovery of Monetary dues, Possession and Transfer of property, Enforcement of Contractual obligations, 138 proceedings, Bail and Anticipatory Bail Petitions, Quash Petitions, Writ Petitions and Consumer related disputes in main hearings and several Interim/Interlocutory petitions and applications.
  • Drafting of Pleadings, Affidavits, Counter/Written Statements; Representations before the Appropriate Forums in Main Petitions and several Interlocutory Applications

  • Legal Representation on behalf of Companies/Institutions/Private Firms and Individuals in matters against the Central and State Government in areas such as Education, Prohibition and Excise, Labour Affairs, Functioning and Compliance with/of Local Authorities Norms/Regulations, Challenging of Land Acquisition, Construction and related Compliance issues, Environmental/Pollution, Public Interest Litigations, Contracts and Tenders Applications.

  • Drafted pleadings and claim statements, conducted witness examination and appeared before the High Court for several Petitions and Interlocutory Applications filed under provisions of the Arbitration and Conciliation Act 1996 (Indian Act

  • Assisted in several Divorce and Child Custody proceedings; Conducted counseling and mediation involving matrimonial issues such as maintenance, Dowry Prohibition act and Domestic violence act

  • Representation before appropriate Arbitral Tribunals (governed by Indian Law) and Courts in Arbitration proceedings on behalf of Companies (State and National Level) and Private parties.
