About Us

Founded in 2016, AGD LAW ASSOCIATES is a boutique law firm of 5 lawyers that specializes in corporate and commercial litigation, arbitration, real estate advisory and documentation. The associates appear before the High Court of Chennai and Madurai Bench, Company Law Boards, National Green Tribunal, Debt Recovery Tribunals, Debt Recovery Appellate Tribunals, Railway Tribunal, Consumer forum and several District Courts. In addition to this, the firm also undertakes real estate related litigation and transactions pertaining to due diligences, title scrutiny and documentation relating to sale, lease and joint development. With office in Chennai, AGD Law Associates also handles work in several cities across the Tamil Nadu and in our country including New Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad and Mumbai by liaising with local lawyers to handle client specific requirements. The members of AGD LAW ASSOCIATES teammates are tailor-made for each individual case assignment so that we do legal research and ensure specialized professional service on the subject matter.
